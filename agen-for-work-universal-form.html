How Q1 x Q2 maps to prospect profile

The combination of current state (maturity) and primary driver (urgency) creates a 2D picture used for routing and prioritization.

Hot lead

Experimenter/Observer + High urgency - They have a problem and they know it. Incident-driven or audit deadline. Fast-track to demo, lead with visibility and audit trail capabilities.

Warm lead

Experimenter + Medium urgency - Agent usage is growing, leadership is paying attention. Needs a governance framework. Lead with shadow AI discovery and policy controls.

Expansion opportunity

AI-Native + Any urgency - Already governing, looking to level up. Lead with advanced capabilities: behavioral analysis, trust scoring, cross-app data flow policies.

Nurture

Observer + Low urgency - Proactive but early. Add to education sequence. Share CISO guide, governance framework content. Re-engage in 30-60 days.
