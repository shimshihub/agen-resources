The situation you're in

You have two bad options. Until now.

AI is everywhere in your company. Finance is building agents. Sales has plugins. Engineering is wiring up MCPs from GitHub. And the request has landed on your desk: make all of this work, safely, without slowing anyone down.

Option 1 — Say no

Lock it down, lose the business

Block the tools. Review every request. Turn IT into the AI police. Business teams route around you. Shadow AI grows anyway, now actively hidden from you. Worst outcome.

Option 2 — Say yes, hope for the best

Let teams wire things up on their own

Credentials in Google Drive. API keys in Slack. Random MCPs from GitHub in production. No inventory, no audit, no way to revoke when an employee leaves. Short-term velocity, long-term nightmare.
